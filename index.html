    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is Productivity Timer?

    Productivity Timer is an all-in-one web tool designed to help you manage your time effectively. It features an event countdown, a Pomodoro timer, and a to-do list, all in one place.

    How do I switch between the countdown and Pomodoro timers?

    Simply use the dropdown menu in the "Select Timer Type" section to choose your preferred timer.

    Can I share my timer with others?

    Yes, generate a shareable link in the "Share Your Timer" section and copy it to your clipboard.

    What happens if I refresh the page?

    Your to-do list items and theme settings are saved in your browser's local storage, but timer progress resets on refresh.
